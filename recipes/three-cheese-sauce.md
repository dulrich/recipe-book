# Three Cheese Sauce #

## Ingredients ##
*	1/4		C	three cheese blend (romano, parmessian, asagio)
*	1		t	cumin powder
*	1		t	onion powder
*	2		t	garlic powder
*	1		t	ground pepper
*	2		t	parsely flakes
*	1		t	basil flakes
*	1		t	ginger powder
*	1-2		C	milk

## Directions ##
1.	Mix dry ingredients in bowl.
2.	Make gravy, use milk to thin.
3.	Stir in cheese/spice mixture.
