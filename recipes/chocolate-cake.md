# Chocolate Cake #

## Ingredients ##
*	1 1/2	C	flour
*	1/3		C	unsweetened cocoa powder
*	1/2		t	baking soda
*	1/2		t	salt
*	1		C	sugar
*	1/2		C	vegetable oil
*	1		C	water (cold)
*	2		t	vanilla
*	2		T	cider vinegar

## Directions ##
1.	Sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, salt, and sugar in medium bowl.
2.	Stir together oil, water, and vanilla. Add wet ingredients to dry. Mix well.
