# California Chicken

## Ingredients
*	2/3		C	flour
*	1/2		t	celery salt
*	1/2		t	garlic powder
*	1/2		t	nutmeg
*	4			whole chicken breats, split, boned, and skinned
*	1		C	pineapple juice
*	1/4		C	butter (melted)
*	1/2		C	soy sauce
*	2		T	sugar

## Directions
1.	Mix flour and spices. Dredge chicken.
2.	Brown chicken in butter. Transfer to baking pan.
3.	Mix pineapple juice, soy sauce, and sugar. Pour over chicken.
4.	Bake at 350Â°F for 60 minutes. Baste every 15 minutes.
