# Enchilada Sauce

## Ingredients
*	1/4		C	oil
*	2		T	flour
*	2		T	chili powder
*	1		C	tomato sauce
*	1 1/2		C	water
*	1/4		t	cumin
*	1/4		t	garlic powder
*	1/4		t	onion salt

## Directions
1.	Make a roux with oil, flour, and chili powder in saucepan
2.	Add water, tomato sauce, and spices.
3.	Bring to low boil and thicken about 10 min, stirring constantly.
