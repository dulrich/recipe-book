# Fajita Seasoning Mix

## Ingredients
*	1		T	cornstarch
*	2		t	chili powder
*	1		t	paprika
*	1		t	sugar
*	1		t	chicken bouillon
*	1/2		t	onion powder
*	1		t	garlic powder
*	1/4		t	cayenne powder
*	1/2		t	cumin

## Directions
1.	Mix all ingredients in bowl.
2.	2 T equals one package fajita seasoning.
