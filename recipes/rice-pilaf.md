# Rice Pilaf

## Ingredients
*	1		C	rice
*	2		T	olive oil
*	1/2		C	orzo
*	2		C	water

*	1		T	chicken bullion
*	1		t	parsely flakes
*	1		t	chives

## Directions
1.	Heat rice, orzo, and oil in medium saucepan over medium heat until golden brown.
2.	Add water, then stir in spices.
3.	Bring boil, then reduce to low heat. Simmer until water is absorbed, about 15-20 minutes.
